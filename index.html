Background +

I’ve always been intrigued by how people interact with information. My journey in Geography taught me that a well-designed map is essentially a user interface for the physical world. I carry this perspective into Data Analytics, where I treat every visualization as a design challenge. To me, UI/UX is the common thread: the art of transforming complex datasets into seamless, intuitive experiences.
